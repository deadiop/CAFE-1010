Welcome to Cafe 1010

Cafe 1010 is designed for creators, students, and travelers who want a balance between productivity and relaxation. Work, stay, and unwind in a calm environment surrounded by nature and comfort.

From ethically sourced beans to freshly prepared meals, every detail at Cafe 1010 is crafted with love. Whether you're here to focus, unwind, or connect — there's a corner waiting for you.
